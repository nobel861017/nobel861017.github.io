About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Graduate Institute of Computer Science and Information Engineering of National Taiwan University. I am currently in the Speech Processing Lab of NTU with Prof. Lin-shan Lee and Prof. Hung-yi Lee as my instructor. My research focuses on enhancing the robustness of speech technologies, particularly in developing methods that are resilient in adverse noisy environments and code-switching scenarios. These advancements are applicable to a wide range of speech processing tasks.
